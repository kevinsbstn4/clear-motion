ClearMotion, the world’s first proactive ride system, transforms the way your car rides and handles.

It does for motion what noise-cancelling does for noise, mitigating road roughness within a

fraction of a second. Relying on a combination of software and hardware, ClearMotion predicts the

road and enables cars to react, giving passengers and drivers unmatched comfort, handling and

stability.
